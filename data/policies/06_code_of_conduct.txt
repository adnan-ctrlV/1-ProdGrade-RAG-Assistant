The Company is committed to maintaining a professional, inclusive, and respectful 
workplace for employees, members, and partners. This Code of Conduct outlines 
expected standards of behavior.

All employees are expected to act with integrity, professionalism, and respect. 
Discrimination, harassment, bullying, or inappropriate behavior of any kind will 
not be tolerated. Employees must comply with all Company policies, applicable laws, 
and safety regulations.

Conflicts of interest must be disclosed, and Company resources should be used 
responsibly and for business purposes only. Employees should protect confidential 
information and avoid actions that could harm the Company’s reputation.

Violations of this Code may result in disciplinary action, up to and including 
termination. Employees are encouraged to report concerns without fear of retaliation.

Upholding this Code ensures a safe, welcoming, and professional environment 
aligned with the Company’s values.
