The Company provides equipment such as computers, mobile devices, access cards, 
and gym-related tools to support job responsibilities. All equipment remains 
Company property unless otherwise stated.

Employees are responsible for the proper use, care, and security of assigned 
equipment. Equipment should be used for business purposes only and must not be 
shared without authorization.

Loss, theft, or damage must be reported immediately. Employees may be held 
financially responsible for damage caused by negligence or misuse.

Upon termination or role change, all Company equipment must be returned 
promptly and in good condition. Failure to return equipment may result in 
deductions where legally permitted.

This policy ensures Company assets are protected and available to support
business operations.
