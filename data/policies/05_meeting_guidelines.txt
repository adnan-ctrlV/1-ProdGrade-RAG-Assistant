Meetings are an important tool for collaboration, decision-making, and alignment. 
This policy establishes standards to ensure meetings are productive, respectful 
of time, and outcome-driven.

All meetings should have a clear agenda, defined objectives, and only required 
participants invited. Meetings should start and end on time. Organizers are 
responsible for sharing relevant materials in advance whenever possible.

Participants are expected to arrive prepared, remain engaged, and minimize 
distractions such as personal phone use. Action items, decisions, and next steps 
should be clearly documented and communicated after the meeting.

Meetings should be scheduled only when necessary; alternatives such as email or 
messaging may be used for simple updates. Standing meetings should be reviewed 
regularly to ensure ongoing value.

By following these guidelines, the Company aims to reduce unnecessary meetings 
while fostering effective communication across teams.
