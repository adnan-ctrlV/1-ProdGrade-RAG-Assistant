The Company conducts regular performance reviews to support employee development, 
accountability, and alignment with business goals. Reviews are typically conducted 
annually, with additional check-ins as needed.

Performance evaluations assess job performance, goal achievement, teamwork, 
professionalism, and adherence to Company values. Employees are encouraged to 
participate actively by providing self-assessments and feedback.

Managers will provide constructive feedback, recognize achievements, and identify 
areas for improvement. Development plans may include training, goal adjustments, 
or additional support.

Performance reviews may influence compensation decisions, promotions, 
and career development opportunities. Consistent underperformance may result in 
corrective action.

This process is designed to foster transparency, growth, and continuous 
improvement across the organization.
