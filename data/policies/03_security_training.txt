The Company is committed to protecting member information, employee data, and 
business systems. All employees are required to participate in mandatory security 
awareness training to reduce the risk of data breaches and operational disruptions.

Security training is required upon onboarding and annually thereafter. 
Training topics include password management, phishing awareness, handling member 
data, secure use of devices, and reporting security incidents. Employees with  
access to sensitive systems may be required to complete additional role-specific training.

Employees must comply with all security protocols taught during training sessions. 
This includes using strong passwords, locking devices when unattended, and 
avoiding unauthorized software or external storage devices.

Failure to complete required security training or comply with security 
standards may result in restricted system access or disciplinary action. 
Suspected security incidents must be reported immediately to management or 
the IT team.

Protecting Company and member data is a shared responsibility. Adherence to 
this policy is essential to maintaining trust and ensuring regulatory compliance.
