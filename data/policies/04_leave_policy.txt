The Company provides paid and unpaid leave benefits to support employee 
well-being while maintaining operational continuity. Leave entitlements vary 
based on employment status, tenure, and applicable labor laws.

Eligible employees accrue paid time off (PTO) for vacation, personal use, 
and short-term illness. Requests for planned leave must be submitted through the 
Company’s scheduling system and approved by a manager in advance. Unplanned 
absences should be reported as soon as possible.

Additional leave types may include sick leave, bereavement leave, parental leave, 
and unpaid leave, subject to approval and legal requirements. Employees may be 
required to provide documentation for certain types of leave.

Unused PTO policies and carryover limits are outlined in the employee handbook. 
Abuse of leave privileges may result in disciplinary action.

The Company complies with all applicable labor laws and encourages employees to 
discuss leave needs openly with management to ensure fair and consistent 
application of this policy.
