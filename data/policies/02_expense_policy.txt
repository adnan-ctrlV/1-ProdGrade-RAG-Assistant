This policy outlines the guidelines for reimbursement of reasonable and necessary 
business-related expenses incurred while performing job duties for the Company.

Reimbursable expenses may include approved travel, meals during business trips, 
training fees, fitness certifications, and minor office or operational supplies. 
All expenses must be directly related to Company business and pre-approved when required.

Employees must submit expense reports within 14 days of incurring the expense, 
accompanied by itemized receipts. Expense submissions should be made through the 
Company’s designated expense management system. Incomplete or late submissions 
may be denied.

Non-reimbursable expenses include personal purchases, alcohol (unless approved 
for client events), fines, penalties, and expenses incurred without proper 
authorization. The Company reserves the right to audit expense reports at any time.

Any misuse of the expense reimbursement process may result in disciplinary 
action, up to and including termination. Questions regarding allowable expenses 
should be directed to the Finance Department prior to making purchases.
