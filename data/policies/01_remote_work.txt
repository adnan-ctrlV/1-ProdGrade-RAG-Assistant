The Company recognizes that certain roles may be performed remotely while 
maintaining high standards of productivity, accountability, and service quality. 
This Remote Work Policy outlines the expectations for employees approved to work 
remotely on a full-time or hybrid basis.

Remote work eligibility is determined based on role requirements, operational 
needs, and performance history. Positions involving direct member interaction, 
facility operations, or equipment handling are generally not eligible for remote 
work. Eligible employees must obtain written approval from their manager prior 
to working remotely.

Remote employees are expected to maintain regular working hours, remain accessible
 via approved communication tools, and attend required meetings. A stable internet 
 connection and a secure, quiet workspace are mandatory. Company data must be 
 accessed only through approved devices and systems.

Performance standards for remote employees are identical to those working on-site.
Failure to meet expectations may result in revocation of remote work privileges. 
The Company reserves the right to modify or discontinue remote work arrangements 
at any time based on business needs.
