This policy governs business-related travel undertaken on behalf of the Company. 
All travel must be necessary, cost-effective, and approved in advance by management.

Employees should use preferred vendors when booking transportation and lodging. 
Reasonable accommodations and economy-class travel are expected unless 
otherwise approved. Travel expenses must comply with the Company’s Expense Policy.

While traveling, employees are expected to conduct themselves professionally and 
adhere to the Code of Conduct. Safety should be prioritized at all times.

Expense reports for travel must be submitted within 14 days of return, along 
with required documentation. Non-compliant expenses may be denied.

The Company reserves the right to modify travel arrangements or cancel trips 
based on business needs or safety considerations.
