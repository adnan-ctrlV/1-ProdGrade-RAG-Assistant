The onboarding process ensures new employees are equipped to succeed in their 
roles. This checklist outlines key onboarding steps and responsibilities.

Prior to the first day, employees must complete required paperwork, provide 
identification, and review core policies. On the first day, employees receive an 
orientation covering Company values, safety procedures, and role expectations.

Training includes system access setup, job-specific instruction, and introductions 
to team members. New hires may be assigned a mentor or supervisor for ongoing support.

Managers are responsible for ensuring training completion and regular check-ins 
during the onboarding period. Employees are encouraged to ask questions and 
seek clarification as needed.

A structured onboarding process promotes engagement, productivity, and long-term success.
