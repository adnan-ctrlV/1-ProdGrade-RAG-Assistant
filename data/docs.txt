Company Remote Work Policy:
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Remote work must be approved by the employee's manager.

Expense Policy:
All expense reports must be submitted by Friday at 5 PM.
Expenses submitted after this deadline will be processed the following week.

Security Policy:
All employees must complete mandatory security training once every quarter.
Failure to complete training may result in restricted system access.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave per year.
Unused leave does not carry over to the next year.
